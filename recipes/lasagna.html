Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. It's the perfect comfort food for family dinners or special occasions.

The recipe takes about 30 minutes to prepare and 45 minutes to bake, serving 8 people generously.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until beef is browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in spaghetti sauce, diced tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. While sauce simmers, cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg.
  6. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Arrange 4 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one third of the ricotta mixture.
  8. Top with 1/4 of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella.
  9. Repeat layers twice, then top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake additional 25 minutes until bubbly.
  11. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.

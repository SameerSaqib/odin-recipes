Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This classic Italian pasta dish features spaghetti tossed with a creamy sauce made from eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's simple, elegant, and incredibly delicious.

Ready in just 20 minutes, this recipe serves 4 people and requires only a handful of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 8 ounces pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook pancetta until crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, egg yolks, Parmesan, Pecorino Romano, and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining spaghetti.
  5. Working quickly, add hot drained spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta.
  6. Remove from heat and pour egg mixture over pasta, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Add pasta water a tablespoon at a time if sauce is too thick.
  8. Season with salt and additional pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
